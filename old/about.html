Why ?

You may ask yourself : why ? Why another CTF ? Isn't there already one ?


The answer is simple : The only current one is rubenwardy's(or derivates). On the one hand, we planned for a long time to create something better. On the other, he banned us for some reasons, and we wanted to beat him in terms of CTF.
